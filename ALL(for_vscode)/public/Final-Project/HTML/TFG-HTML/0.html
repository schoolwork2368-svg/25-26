    • Welcome to TFG

    TerraFirmaGreg is one of Minecraft's many modpacks. TerraFirmaGreg enhances the average Minecraft's survival experience. Embarking on a journey through a vast world with untamed wilderness, you must learn to adapt and thrive using the resources around you.

    TFG aims to be one of if not the most realistic Minecraft mod with some sci-fi elements. It has a wide range of features that could help you flourish; or be your downfall.
